How to Perform a Proper Romanian Dead Lift

  • Stand with feet hip-width apart, slight bend in knees
  • Push hips backward (like closing a door with your butt)
  • Keep your back straight and chest up
  • Lower weight along your legs until you feel hamstring stretch
  • Drive hips forward and squeeze glutes to stand up
  • Keep the weight close to your body throughout
