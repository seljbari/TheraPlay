How to Perform a Proper Split Squat

  • Start with your feet hip-width apart
  • Keep your back straight and lower your left knee (back leg) toward the floor
  • Before the left knee (back leg) touches the floor, press down on your right foot to pull back on the right knee
  • Push the top of your left foot into the bench and return to the starting position
